Our Story

Built on Passion,
Driven by Purpose

Rooibaard Construction is a 100% black youth owned local Engineering & Mining services contractor operating in Postmasburg, Northern Cape. We provide complete engineering solutions for both private and public sectors.

Founded in 2015 by Mandilakhe Vincent Rooibaard, a young entrepreneur from Postmasburg with extensive experience in the construction and steel industries, our company has grown to become a trusted name in civil engineering and mining services throughout the Northern Cape region.

From modest beginnings, we have secured contracts with industry giants like AngloAmerican and Bidvest — a testament to our unwavering commitment to quality, safety, and timely delivery.

Our Vision

"To provide affordable and sustainable quality engineering & construction services, striving to be the most successful construction company in the region."

Our Mission

"To operate a disciplined company in Civil Engineering & Mining services, leading the way to improvement for prompt and efficient services."

Milestones

Our Journey

From a vision in 2015 to a trusted Northern Cape contractor — every milestone marks our commitment to growth and excellence.

2015

Company Founded

Rooibaard Construction was officially registered as a 100% black youth owned company with a vision to transform the construction industry in the Northern Cape. CK No: 2015/236912/07.

2017

First Major Contract

Secured our first major civil engineering contract, establishing our reputation for quality work in road construction and infrastructure development across the Northern Cape.

2019

Mining Services Expansion

Expanded our portfolio to include specialized mining support services — plant fabrication, pipeline installation and technical services — working with major mining companies in the region.

2021

BBBEE Level 1 Achievement

Achieved BBBEE Level 1 certification, demonstrating our commitment to transformation and empowerment in South Africa's construction sector — one of the highest ratings possible.

2024

Continued Growth

With over 9 years of proven performance, Rooibaard Construction continues to expand its footprint across the Northern Cape, delivering world-class engineering and mining services.
