Common Questions

FAQ

How do I get a quote for my project?

You can reach us by phone at 082 422 7601, WhatsApp the same number, or email us at sales@rooibaardconstruction.com. We'll arrange a site visit or consultation to provide an accurate, detailed quote.

What areas do you service?

We are based in Postmasburg and primarily serve the Northern Cape region, including Kathu and Kimberley. We also take on projects across a wider area — contact us to discuss your location.

Are you BBBEE certified?

Yes. Rooibaard Construction holds a BBBEE Level 1 rating, the highest possible. We are also registered with CIDB, NHBRC, and are COIDA compliant.

Do you handle both civil and mining projects?

Absolutely. We specialise in both Civil Engineering (roads, dams, infrastructure, HDPE pipe work) and Mining Services (plant fabrication, coded welding, pipeline installation, technical services).

What is your typical project turnaround time?

Turnaround time depends on project scope and complexity. We are committed to realistic timelines and clear communication throughout every project. Contact us for a more specific estimate.
